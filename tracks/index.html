Build Civic Apps

There is no shortage of organizations in need of coders or designers who can help them build civic apps. To start with, Code for BTV will be working with Big Heavy World to build a set of web apps that will help preserve and promote Vermont-made music. Let's work together to prototype some of these apps!

  • Vermont Live Music Venue Directory (Big Heavy World)
  • Vermont Artist Directory (Big Heavy World)
  • Interactive Calendar (Big Heavy World)
  • Coordinating Green-Up Day Events (Green Up VT)

Reuse Civic Apps

Hackers have already created dozens of open source civic apps through Code for America. Many citizens have reused and repurposed these apps for their cities. Let's work together to reuse some of these civic apps for your community!

  • Adopt-a-Hydrant
  • CityGroups
  • SnapFresh
  • MediaWiki for BTV
  • WordPress
    • Deerfield Valley Rescue Squad
    • Bethel Food Pantry
    • Woodstock Area Job Bank
  • Public Forum (Big Heavy World)

Open Data

Cities across the country are starting to see the benefits of opening their data to hackers. What apps could you build if your local town's data were more open? Let's work together to catalog and expand the amount of open data available from municipal governments!

  • Smart Grid
  • Vermont Fish & Wildlife Stocking Schedule
  • DataCouch
  • Lakecraft: Porting the Champlain Basin to Minecraft (ECHO)
