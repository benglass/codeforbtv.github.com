Improve Your Neighborhood and Community through Technology

Join thousands of participants from around the world for National Day of Civic Hacking at Generator. Organized by Code for BTV, the Burlington event will bring together software developers, technology integrators, designers, makers and civic hackers to improve our neighborhoods and communities through technology.

You don't need to be an expert to participate; you just have to care about your neighborhood and community. Participants will work on projects in the following areas:

  • Food Systems & Agriculture
  • Renewable & Sustainable Energy
  • Arts & Culture
  • Human-powered Recreation
